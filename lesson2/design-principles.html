White Space and Clean Design

Alexander Sakhnenko

https://shpro.info/

Alexander Sakhnenko's website design reflect a great use of white Space and clean design. You can see on image on the background and a big title of his name. The rest is clean. it feels like this website is breathing.

Contrast

Tradingview

https://www.tradingview.com/

In this tradingview website I can really see the contrast between the background and the foreground. The choice of color (here the image) is perfect to reflect the foreground and make it easy to focus and read.

Alignment

Jamie Oliver

https://www.jamieoliver.com/

I believe a good food website must have a good alignment with its items. This website has a good center horizontal alignment. Also the photo looks like they have been put into a grid.
